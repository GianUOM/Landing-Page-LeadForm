Features

Smart Form Builder

Build multi-step forms with conditional logic, real-time validation, and conversion-optimized layouts in minutes — no code needed.

AI Lead Scoring

Automatically qualify and score every lead based on behavior, firmographics, and engagement signals before they even speak to your team.

Instant Routing

Route the right leads to the right rep automatically. Set rules by industry, deal size, territory, or any custom field you define.
